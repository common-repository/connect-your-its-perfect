WP Connect Your Itsperfect Plugin is a simple, advanced, easy to setup Its Perfect ERP wordpress plugin.

== Description ==

WP Its Perfect plugin is a simple, advanced, easy to setup Its Perfect wordpress plugin for Integrating complete eCommerce features.
It allows user to sync list of catagories, products, product attributes for simple and variations. It allows you to schedule sync jobs for stock, price or any ohter custom attribute
It allows user to sync the orders back to ERP both manually or automatically

Ordered list:

1. Generate Its Perfect wordpress form with or without web forms.
2. Supports Contact Form 7 plugin.
3. Use Its Perfect wordpress plugin forms in Page, Post, popups, optins or Widgets.
4. Captures potential leads to Its Perfect CRM, SugarCRM, Zoho CRM and Salesforce.
5. Redirects User to the desired page after form submission.
6. Notification on success or failure of lead capture.
7. Its Perfect plugins convert new Users as your CRM contact.
8. Its Perfect wordpress ecommerce integration



== Installation ==
To connect your wordpress website with Its Perfect ERP [https://www.itsperfect-solutions.com/] follow the steps below

1. Upload "its perfect erp" to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress

You will find ‘Its Perfect Sync’ menu in your WordPress admin panel.
